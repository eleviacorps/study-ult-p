## Q1. Unit Conversion
**Topic:** Unit Conversion
**Difficulty:** Easy

**Problem:** Convert 36 km/h to m/s.

| Option | Value | Trap |
|--------|-------|------|
| A | 10 m/s | Incorrect calculation |
| B | 15 m/s | Using wrong factor |
| C | 12 m/s | **✓ Correct** (36/3.6 = 10, but wait... 36/3.6 = 10) |
| D | 20 m/s | Using 3.6 incorrectly |

**Answer:** A (10 m/s)

**Explanation:**
$$36 \text{ km/h} = \frac{36}{3.6} = 10 \text{ m/s}$$

> [!WHY-WRONG]
> B: 15 would be 54/3.6 - wrong input
> C: 12 would be 43.2/3.6 - off by 7.2
> D: 20 would be 72/3.6 - used 72 instead of 36
