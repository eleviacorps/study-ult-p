## All Formulas Organized by Topic

### Electric Charge

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Charge from electrons | q = ne | Find charge from number of electrons | n must be integer |
| Total charge | Q = Σqᵢ | Sum of multiple charges | Algebraic sum |
| Elementary charge | e = 1.602 × 10⁻¹⁹ C | Always | Fundamental constant |

---

### Coulomb's Law

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Force magnitude | F = k\|q₁q₂\|/r² | Point charges in vacuum | k = 9 × 10⁹ |
| Force vector | F = kq₁q₂/r² r̂ | With direction | For q₁q₂ > 0, repulsive |
| Coulomb constant | k = 1/(4πε₀) | Always | ε₀ = 8.85 × 10⁻¹² |

---

### Electric Field

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Field definition | E = F/q | Definition | q is test charge |
| Point charge field | E = kQ/r² | Single charge | Only at distance r |
| Multiple charges | E = ΣEᵢ | Several charges | Vector sum! |
| Field from dipole (axis) | E = 2p/(4πε₀r³) | r >> a | Far from dipole |
| Field from dipole (equator) | E = p/(4πε₀r³) | r >> a | Half of axial |

---

### Gauss's Law

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Gauss's law | Φ = q/ε₀ | Any closed surface | Only enclosed charge |
| Flux | Φ = ∮E·dS | General flux | Surface integral |
| Infinite line | E = λ/(2πε₀r) | Long wire | Cylindrical symmetry |
| Infinite sheet | E = σ/(2ε₀) | Large plane | Independent of distance |
| Sphere (outside) | E = kQ/r² | r > R | Like point charge |
| Sphere (inside) | E = 0 | r < R | Hollow shell |
| Solid sphere (inside) | E = kQr/R³ | r < R | Linear with r |

---

### Electric Dipole

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Dipole moment | p = q × 2a | Definition | Direction: -q to +q |
| Torque | τ = pE sinθ | Uniform field | Max at 90° |
| Potential energy | U = -pE cosθ | Uniform field | Min at alignment |
| Axial field | E = 2p/(4πε₀r³) | Far points | 1/r³ |
| Equatorial field | E = p/(4πε₀r³) | Far points | Half of axial |

---

### Continuous Charge Distribution

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Linear density | λ = Q/L | Line charge | C/m |
| Surface density | σ = Q/A | Sheet charge | C/m² |
| Volume density | ρ = Q/V | Volume charge | C/m³ |
| Field from ring (axis) | E = kQx/(R²+x²)³ᐟ² | On axis | At distance x |

---

### Flux

| Topic | Formula | When to Use | Limitation |
|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Flux element | dΦ = E·dS | Small area | cosθ matters |
| Uniform field | Φ = EA cosθ | Constant E | θ = angle with normal |
| Closed surface | Φ = q/ε₀ | Gauss's law | Net flux |

## Quick Reference

### Key Constants
- k = 9 × 10⁹ N·m²/C²
- ε₀ = 8.85 × 10⁻¹² C²/N·m²
- e = 1.602 × 10⁻¹⁹ C

### Important Conversions
- 1 μC = 10⁻⁶ C
- 1 nC = 10⁻⁹ C
- 1 cm = 0.01 m
- 1 mm = 0.001 m

### Unit Relationships
- N/C = V/m (equivalent!)
- 1 N = 1 J/m
- 1 C = 1 A·s

## Exception Cases

| Situation | Use This Formula | NOT This |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Point charge | E = kQ/r² | E = constant |
| Dipole (far) | E ∝ 1/r³ | E ∝ 1/r² |
| Inside shell | E = 0 | E = kQ/r² |
| Infinite sheet | E = σ/2ε₀ | E = σ/ε₀ |
| Infinite wire | E ∝ 1/r | E ∝ 1/r² |
