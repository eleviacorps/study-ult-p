## Q1. Coulomb's Force Calculation
**Topic:** [[Coulomb's Law]]
**Subtopic:** Force between point charges
**Difficulty:** Easy
**Marks:** 4

### Given:
- Charge q₁ = +2 μC
- Charge q₂ = +3 μC
- Distance r = 30 cm

### Find:
Electric force on q₂ due to q₁

### Solution:
$$F = k \frac{q_1 q_2}{r^2} = \frac{9 \times 10^9 \times (2 \times 10^{-6}) \times (3 \times 10^{-6})}{(0.3)^2} = 0.6 \text{ N}$$

### Answer:
✅ **0.6 N (Repulsive, away from q₁)**
