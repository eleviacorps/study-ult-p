## 4. Measuring Instruments

### Vernier Caliper
- **Least Count (LC):** LC = 1 mm / (number of vernier divisions)
- **Reading:** Main scale reading + (vernier division × LC)
- **Zero Error:** If vernier zero right of main zero → positive error (subtract); if left → negative error (add)

### Screw Gauge
- **Least Count (LC):** LC = Pitch / (number of circular divisions)
- **Reading:** Main scale reading + (circular division × LC)
- **Zero Error:** Similar to vernier

## 5. Error Analysis Formulas

### Absolute Error
- Single measurement: Δx = LC/2
- Multiple measurements: Δx = (max - min)/2

### Relative Error
$$\text{Relative Error} = \frac{\Delta x}{x}$$

### Percentage Error
$$\text{Percentage Error} = \frac{\Delta x}{x} \times 100\%$$

### Error Propagation

| Operation | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| z = x ± y | Δz = Δx + Δy |
| z = xy | Δz/z = Δx/x + Δy/y |
| z = x/y | Δz/z = Δx/x + Δy/y |
| z = xⁿ | Δz/z = |n| × (Δx/x) |

## 6. Significant Figures Rules

### In Multiplication/Division
- Result has same significant figures as quantity with fewest significant figures

### In Addition/Subtraction
- Result has same decimal places as quantity with fewest decimal places

## 7. Important Conversions

### Length
- 1 km = 1000 m
- 1 cm = 10 mm = 10⁻² m
- 1 mm = 10⁻³ m
- 1 μm = 10⁻⁶ m
- 1 nm = 10⁻⁹ m
- 1 Å = 10⁻¹⁰ m
- 1 fm = 10⁻¹⁵ m

### Mass
- 1 kg = 1000 g
- 1 g = 10⁻³ kg
- 1 u (atomic mass unit) = 1.66 × 10⁻²⁷ kg

### Time
- 1 hour = 3600 s
- 1 day = 86400 s
- 1 year = 3.15 × 10⁷ s

### Angle
- 180° = π rad
- 1 rad = 180°/π

### Pressure
- 1 atm = 1.01 × 10⁵ Pa
- 1 bar = 10⁵ Pa

### Energy
- 1 cal = 4.184 J
- 1 eV = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ J
- 1 kWh = 3.6 × 10⁶ J

## 8. Key Formulas

### Measurement
$$\text{Measured Value} = \text{Numerical Value} \times \text{Unit}$$

### Least Count
$$\text{LC} = \frac{\text{Value of one main scale division}}{\text{Number of vernier divisions}}$$

### Dimensional Formula
$$Q = L^a M^b T^c \Theta^d I^e J^f N^g$$

## 10. Calculus Quick Reference

### Derivatives
- d/dx(xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹
- d/dx(sin x) = cos x
- d/dx(cos x) = -sin x
- d/dx(eˣ) = eˣ
- d/dx(ln x) = 1/x

### Integrals
- ∫xⁿ dx = xⁿ⁺¹/(n+1) + C (n ≠ -1)
- ∫(1/x) dx = ln|x| + C
- ∫sin x dx = -cos x + C
- ∫cos x dx = sin x + C
