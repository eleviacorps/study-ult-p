## Q1. Fundamental Quantity Identification
**Topic:** [[Fundamental and Derived Quantities]]
**Difficulty:** Easy

### Given:
Which of the following is NOT a fundamental quantity?

### Options:
A) Length
B) Force
C) Time
D) Temperature

### Answer:
✅ **B) Force**

### Explanation:
Force is a derived quantity (MLT⁻²). Length, time, and temperature are fundamental quantities.
